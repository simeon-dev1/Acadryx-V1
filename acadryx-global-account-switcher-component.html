Component Usage & Behavior

  • Student Account: Always active during enrollment. Shows current academic tools and resources.
  • Alumni Account: Locked until graduation confirmation. Shows tooltip explanation on hover.
  • Automatic Transition: Alumni account activates automatically when graduation is confirmed by the institution.
  • State Persistence: Once alumni account is active, users can switch between student (read-only) and alumni (full access) views.

This component is designed to be placed in the global header or footer of the Acadryx application, providing consistent account switching functionality across all pages.
