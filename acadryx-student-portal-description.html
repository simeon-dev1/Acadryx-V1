Portal Overview

Your Academic Hub

The Acadryx Student Portal provides current students with access to school resources, course materials, projects, and a preview of alumni features that will unlock upon graduation.

During your enrollment, use the portal to manage your academic journey, stay updated with school announcements, and connect with your educational community.

Key Features

School Announcements

View important updates, event schedules, and academic deadlines from your institution.

Classmate Connections

Connect with peers in your classes and join study groups within your school network.

School Magazine

Access the latest editions of your school's publications and student work highlights.

Academic Progress

Track your coursework, grades, and academic milestones throughout your enrollment.

Future Alumni Access

Preview alumni features that will automatically unlock when your graduation is confirmed.

Project Submissions

Submit assignments and projects through the portal for faculty review and grading.
