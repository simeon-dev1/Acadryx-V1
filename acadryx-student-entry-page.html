About School Codes

Your school code is a unique identifier that connects you to your educational institution within the Acadryx platform.

  • Obtain your code from your school administrator, teacher, or enrollment materials
  • Each code is specific to your school and academic year
  • Codes are case-sensitive and typically include both letters and numbers
  • If you've lost your code, contact your school's administration office

Alumni Access Timeline

Alumni features unlock automatically after graduation. As a current student, you have access to academic resources, course materials, and school communication tools.

Upon graduation confirmation by your school, your account will automatically transition to alumni status with these benefits:

  • Permanent account access (no expiration)
  • Lifelong networking with classmates
  • Career resources and job boards
  • Continued access to certain institutional resources
  • Alumni event invitations

Note: Alumni-specific features remain locked until your graduation is officially recorded in your school's system.
