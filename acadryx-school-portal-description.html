Portal Overview

Institutional Management Platform

The Acadryx School Portal provides educational institutions with enterprise-grade tools to manage multiple branches, administrative staff, student enrollment, and the transition to alumni networks.

Designed for schools of all sizes, from single-campus institutions to multi-branch organizations, with full compliance for educational data protection.

Key Features

Branch Management

Create and manage multiple campuses, departments, and administrative units from a single dashboard.

Staff Roles & Permissions

Assign different access levels to administrators, teachers, and support staff with granular controls.

Student Enrollment

Manage student admissions, class assignments, academic records, and graduation processing.

Announcement Publishing

Create and distribute school announcements, events, and important updates to students and parents.

School Magazine

Publish digital school magazines and newsletters with distribution to students, parents, and alumni.

Alumni Management

Automatically transition graduates to alumni status and manage lifelong institutional connections.
