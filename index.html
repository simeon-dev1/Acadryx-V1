Alumni Account Access

Alumni accounts are created automatically upon graduation. There is no separate registration process for alumni status. When a student completes their program, their account automatically transitions to alumni status with additional features and permanent access.

If you are a graduate trying to access alumni features but your account hasn't been transitioned yet, please contact your institution's administration to verify your graduation status.

Important Notice

Alumni-specific features (networking, career resources, giving portals) are locked until graduation verification is complete. Current students can access these features once their graduation is confirmed by their institution.
